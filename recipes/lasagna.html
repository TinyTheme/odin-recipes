Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with meat sauce (ragù), white sauce (béchamel or ricotta), and cheese. It is typically baked until the cheeses melt and bubble into a golden crust.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Creamy cheese filling

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Mix the Cheese Filling
  3. Cook the Noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake and Serve
